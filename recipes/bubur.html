bubur

Description

This food is called bubur, it is famous among indonesian people, it is very delicious

Sometimes it can be spicy and sweet

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Meat
  • Chilly
  • Rice

Steps

  1. You have to cook the flour
  2. You have to cook the meat
  3. You have to add the Chilly
  4. You have to add the Rice
