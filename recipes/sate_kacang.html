sate

Description

This food is called sate kacang, it is famous among indonesian people, it is very delicious

It is sweet and delicious taste

  • Chilly
  • Beans

Steps

  2. You have to cook the meat
  3. You have to add the Chilly
  4. You have to add the Beans
